# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

Everyone who participates in the **rcmvn** project — contributors, maintainers, and users — is expected to make participation a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, experience level, nationality, or background.

---

## Our Standards

**Encouraged behavior:**
- Being respectful and constructive in discussions
- Welcoming newcomers and helping them get started
- Giving and accepting feedback gracefully
- Focusing on what is best for the community

**Not acceptable:**
- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Dismissing or belittling someone's contribution
- Spam, off-topic promotion, or trolling
- Publishing others' private information without permission

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in all project spaces — GitHub Issues, Pull Requests, Discussions, and any other official communication channel.

---

## Enforcement

Violations can be reported by opening a private issue or contacting the author directly via GitHub: [@rckukaswal](https://github.com/rckukaswal).

All reports will be reviewed and handled with discretion. The maintainer reserves the right to remove comments, close issues, or ban users who violate this code.
